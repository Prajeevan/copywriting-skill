# Copywriting Skill

A reusable [Agent Skill](https://skills.sh) that teaches AI agents to write persuasive, high-converting copy — ads, headlines, landing pages, email, VSL/short-form video scripts, product descriptions, and CTAs.

Built on classic direct-response frameworks (Ogilvy, Hopkins, Sugarman, the Wall Street Journal "Two Young Men" ad, Cialdini) as distilled by **Sam Parr** (founder of *The Hustle*, co-host of *My First Million*), plus a section drawn directly from **Joseph Sugarman's *The Adweek Copywriting Handbook***.

> **Credit:** The Sam Parr material is distilled from four of his interviews (Sweat Equity, This Week in Startups, Content & Conversations, and a live workshop). The Sugarman section comes from *The Adweek Copywriting Handbook*. Full source list with video URLs is in the "Credit & source" section of [`SKILL.md`](skills/copywriting/SKILL.md). This repo is a study aid; all original insight belongs to those authors.

## What's inside

- **Core philosophy** — "no such thing as too long, only too boring"; write for the *right* reader, not everyone; niches make riches
- **Finding the idea** — the 3 content buckets, mining Reddit/comments/news for what people already care about, the "one line = the whole piece" hook method
- **Headlines & hooks** — the curiosity gap, headline-as-spoken-sentence, sub-headline that continues the story, search-vs-social headlines, emotion targeting (awe/anger share, sadness doesn't)
- **Slippery slope body copy** — micro-commitments, the Trojan-horse product reveal
- **Storytelling** — customer = hero / brand = guide, "but/therefore" not "and-then", the story premium, framebreaking
- **A worked AIDA ad** (= hook/meat/CTA), objection handling, proof & social proof
- **Landing pages & product copy** — above-the-fold = a feeling, the feature death march, founder letters, the About-page story
- **The "forgotten text"**, **using AI as a sparring partner**, and **distribution**
- **Mechanics** — rhythm, active voice, no adverbs, no em-dashes/semicolons, ~4th–7th-grade reading level
- **Copy Work** — the by-hand imitation practice + swipe files
- **Joseph Sugarman's system** — his axioms, the slippery slide, sell-a-concept, and all 31 psychological triggers as a checklist
- A pre-ship checklist

## Install

```bash
npx skills add Prajeevan/copywriting-skill
```

Then ask your agent to write or sharpen any piece of marketing/sales copy and it will apply these frameworks.
